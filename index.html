Our Mission

In an ever-changing world, one thing has remained the same for centuries, poverty. In the same regard, there are always people to help bridge that gap. That is where Whole Harvest Company is introduced. We would like to provide quality food and educational opportunities to our fellow humans.

By providing healthy natural foods, we hope to inspire people to want to begin growing their own, and then help someone else in the same way. There are families everywhere who need help, and it should be our honor as fellow humans to assist where we can.

Interested in Volunteering?

Volunteer for Whole Harvest through the Outpost Mission — PLANT'D is about connecting with God and people by learning sustainability through gardening. With God's blessing and our hard work, we will be planting fruits and vegetables to help those in need. Whether you are just learning or a master gardener, we invite you to come share breakfast, fellowship, and digging in the dirt! The benefit of this group is to grow together, learn, and teach each other ways to help sustain themselves by utilizing the blessings God has given us.
‍
The goal of this group is to help feed people in need! We will be engaging other outposts, local businesses, and shelters to find the best ways to deliver food to those in need. Ultimately, we hope to inspire you to start more Plant'd groups so that more mouths can be fed.
