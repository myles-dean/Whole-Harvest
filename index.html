The Homeless Task Force fosters a coordinated system and holistic approach to eliminate barriers, achieve long-term housing stability and empower individuals experiencing homelessness or at risk of homelessness, thereby supporting the City of Anderson and it's community.

Our Vision is to Empower individuals experiencing homelessness or at risk of homelessness by fostering proactive partnerships, ensuring that every resident of the CIty of Anderson has access to safe, stable housing and a supportive community.
